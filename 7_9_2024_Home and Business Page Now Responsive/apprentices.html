The first step to becoming a MAXX Potential Apprentice is to sign up for our Career Lab. This is a free course that is open to anyone interested in pursuing a career in technology. During the workshop, you will:

  1. Explore different technology career paths
  2. Engage in hands-on problem solving
  3. Meet IT leaders

All Apprentices are compensated for their work. As you learn new skills, you will advance through the various levels of our program, consequently building a strong portfolio of real projects and earning a paycheck. You’ll have the opportunity to work on projects involving:

  • Quality Assurance and Testing
  • Application Maintenance and Support
  • Information Security
  • Software Development
  • Data Management and Reporting

Each IT Apprentice will advance through our program at their own pace. It is our goal for you to feel confident in your skills. Then, we work to get you into a high-paying long-term opportunity.
